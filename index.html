Our Mission

To create accessible safety tech for blind, elderly and people with disabilities to stay independent and safe.

How It Helps

Ultrasonic sensors prevent collisions, gas sensor detects leaks, motion sensor increases awareness and buzzer alerts instantly.

Technology

Built with Arduino Uno R3, ultrasonic, gas sensors and rechargeable battery. Portable and easy to use.
